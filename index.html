About

I am an experienced personal trainer with a comprehensive background in high-intensity interval training (HIIT), community engagement, and military discipline. As a group fitness instructor at Functional 45, I specialize in HIIT, designing workouts that push my clients to their limits while ensuring safety and maximizing results. My dedication to fitness extends into my role as a group instructor for adult communities, where I bring enthusiasm, motivation, and a tailored approach to suit the unique needs of each participant. My approach to training is deeply influenced by my experience as an Air Force veteran, where I developed a robust understanding of military fitness training. This diverse range of experiences makes me a versatile and effective trainer, committed to helping my clients achieve their fitness goals with a focus on strength, endurance, and resilience.
